### 2

##### Absurd as it might seem to me, a thousand miles from any human habitation and in danger of death, I took out of my pocket a sheet of paper and my fountain-pen.

1. "Absurd as it might seem to me" - 让步状语从句，倒装结构，相当于"Although it might seem absurd to me"
2. "a thousand miles from any human habitation and in danger of death" - 插入的状语，描述当时的环境状况
3. "I took out of my pocket a sheet of paper and my fountain-pen" - 主句，宾语后置（正常语序是took a sheet of paper and my fountain-pen out of my pocket）
- **fountain-pen**：自来水笔（较老式译法）或钢笔
---
##### over once more
**彻底地再过一遍；重新仔细检查**

##### If you please——draw me a sheep...
**请你……给我画一只绵羊，好吗？"**
在 **"If you please"** 这个固定表达中，**"please" 是动词**（意为"使高兴/满意"），相当于 **"if it pleases you"**（如果这合您意的话/如果您愿意）。

---
##### He bent his head over the drawing
他低头看那幅画
**Bent his head over：** 字面意思是“把头弯向……上方”，引申为“低头看”或“俯身于”

### 3

##### it take me a long time to do 
- **It**：形式主语（Formal Subject）。
- **takes**：谓语动词（及物动词，意为“需要/花费”）。
- **me**：间接宾语（Indirect Object）。
- **a long time**：**直接宾语**（Direct Object）。
- **to do...**：真正主语（Real Subject）。
---
##### It was from words dropped by chance that, little by little, everything was revealed to me.

这里的 **that** 是**强调句型**（It is/was... that...）中的连接词，没有实际意义，仅起结构作用。
翻译时通常可以译为：**“正是...”**。
**整句意思：** **正是**通过那些偶然间流露出的言语，我才逐渐了解了一切。

---
##### And the little prince broke into a lovely peal of laughter, which irritated me very much
- **break into**：突然开始。通常接 laughter（大笑）、tears（流泪）或 applause（鼓掌）。
- **a peal of laughter**：一阵响亮的笑声。`peal` 原指钟声齐鸣，用在这里形容笑声清脆、响亮且连贯。

---
##### That is so. And if you are good I will give you a string, too, so that you can tie him during the day, and a post to tie him to.

1. "That is so."
这是“主系表”结构。这里的 **so** 是形容词，意为“确实如此”、“是真的”。这是一种比较简洁、地道的表达方式。
2. "so that" 引导的目的状语从句
3. “确实如此。如果你听话，我还会给你一根绳子，好让你白天能把它拴住，另外再给你一根桩子。”
